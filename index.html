About Me

I am a first-year Master's student at Beijing Technology and Business University (BTBU), supervised by Prof. Xiaochuan Wang. My research focuses on Point Cloud Quality Assessment (PCQA), aiming to develop robust methods for evaluating and improving the quality of 3D point cloud data.

My current work involves exploring novel deep learning architectures for point cloud processing and quality evaluation, with particular emphasis on developing metrics that align with human perception of visual quality.

Research Interests

Point Cloud Quality Assessment

Developing objective and subjective metrics for evaluating the perceptual quality of 3D point clouds.

3D Vision

Techniques for processing, analyzing, and understanding 3D point cloud data for various applications.

Deep Learning

Applying deep neural networks to point cloud processing tasks including classification, segmentation, and quality assessment.
